# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 13, 2026**

## Introduction

Neryad ("I", "me", or "my") built PocketList as a free app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page informs you regarding my policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use my Service.

## No Data Collection

PocketList does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data you create within the app — including shopping lists, item names, prices, quantities, categories, and preferences — is stored **exclusively on your device** using local storage (SQLite and SharedPreferences).

I do **not** operate servers, databases, or backend infrastructure. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it via your device's native sharing features (e.g., exporting a CSV file or sharing via the system share sheet).

## Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you choose to enter your name in the profile section, that information is stored locally on your device only. It is not transmitted to me or any third party.

## Log Data

PocketList does **not** collect log data, crash reports, or usage analytics. There are no third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Crashlytics) integrated into the app.

## Cookies

This Service does not use cookies explicitly. The app does not display web content that would set cookies beyond what your device's operating system manages.

## Third-Party Services

PocketList does not employ any third-party companies to process your data. The app has no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or social media SDKs.

The only external network requests the app makes are:
- Checking for app updates via the App Store or Google Play Store (handled by the `upgrader` package)
- Opening URLs you explicitly tap on (e.g., links to Terms, Privacy Policy, or author's website)

## Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with anyone. Since no data is collected, there is nothing to share.

## Your Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected about you
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
- Receive equal service and price when exercising your privacy rights

Since PocketList does not collect personal information, these rights are inherently respected. To exercise any privacy rights, contact me at neryadg@gmail.com.

## Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

## Security

I value your trust. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's own security measures. I recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using device-level protections such as passcodes or biometric authentication.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.
